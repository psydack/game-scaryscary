# Scary Scary

Scary Scary is a game you must to prove you are a monster, not a cute monster like other monsters naming you. 
- Arrows to move your player 
- Space to scare 

*Wait for right moment to scare.*


GO THERE AND SHOW WHO IS THE MONSTER!
